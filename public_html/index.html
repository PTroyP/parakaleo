Focus on What Matters Most
— Your Time

Running a company isn’t easy. (As a small business owner, I know this firsthand!) Even so-called “side hustles” can demand constant attention. And if you’re a solopreneur, managing various business elements—marketing, customer service, invoices, taxes, etc.—can be overwhelming. But there’s good news too.

As the person in charge, you get to pick who you work with and how you run things. Many try the DIY route because it promises an initial cost savings. But what you save in money you spend in time—a resource that, unlike money, is not renewable. Engaging a specialist, on the other hand, can give you the freedom to focus on what you do best and set you on a path to greater prosperity.

“Parakaleo” is a Greek term meaning “to come alongside and help,” and that’s exactly what I do. When you work with me, you get more than a bookkeeping service: you get an adviser and ally with expert knowledge of the real estate world. My mission is to help hardworking business owners attain financial peace of mind and reach new heights of success.
